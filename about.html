About

Purpose of the Website

TL;DR

  • Simplify the deckbuilding process for beginners.
  • Allow current players to quickly create a deck for testing/trying a new clan that they have no experience in playing.

Card Anatomy

Cards contain up to 19 parts

It is difficult to keep up as a beginner as most of the parts are hidden.

Is this a good card?

It is difficult to tell without putting in hours into the game.

It is also clan dependant as this may do well against Scorpion but it might not do well against Dragon

Glory

Glory is an important aspect of deckbuilding.

When Honored, this value is added to the Military and Political values of the character

When Dishonored, this value is subtracted from the Military and Political values of the character

When deckbuilding it is important to consider the clan you are facing as this can impact if your cards are likely to be Honored or Dishonored.

Trait

Traits are important for some clans and less for others.

Phoenix for example need many Spells and Shugenja. This is because they have many cards that have good interactivity with these traits.

Clans like Dragon or Crab care less about traits.

Ability

Card abilities is one of the most important factors in strength/weakness of a card

Some cards are very clear in figuring their strength, however some cards may become more powerful as the living card game progresses and more cards come into the game.
